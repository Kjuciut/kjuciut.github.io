How it all started?

As I like to say, it was meant for me to do makeup. I have got into it when ballroom dancing rules made me get my makeup done. (If you are not interested in makeup you can jump right into dance page.;)) My mom signed me up for a day camp where I was supposed to learn some beauty secrets. That camp made me realize - I love to do makeup. I was about 14 or 15 years old, so i could not start my makeup career this early. (Who would go to get their makeup done to a 15 years old teenager?)

When I was 18 years old I signed for my first professional make up courses. After finishing basics' course I took one year gap and went to pro courses. Now I can say that I am a professional makeup artist. I do makeup for myself and for others too.
