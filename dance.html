First steps

I remember as if it was yesterday. I am sitting in the kitchen, watching TV and on the news a lady talks about a ballroom dancing dance studio - Zuvedra. Next day my moms takes me there to sign me up, after 5 months of dancing - I am standing in the first line, telling kids around me to get in their places, music starts playing and we begin to dance. This was my first show - Christmas in our dance studio. I was 5 years old when I first participated in a dance class. I fell in love with dance from the very first step and it grows till now.

Second chapter

When my dance partner left Klaipeda and me, I could not find another one, so I decided to try something new. I wanted to challange myself, so I wrote to one of the street dance studios' owner and she invited me. If anyone would have told me that I will learn this much, not only different styles of dance, but about myself. That I will meet new awesome people, that are now my close friends. And that I will participate in so many events. I would say that they are lying. SFINX became my second Family and I couldn not ask for more. <3
